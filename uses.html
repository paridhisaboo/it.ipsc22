As Useful As a Phone!

Meet the new GPS Tracking Bracelet!
Its like a fitbit, but even better! With its health benefits, cloud usage, analytical skills, application in the field of education and most of all, its live tracking! At any given point, it can give the precise co-ordinates of the user.
Such an innovative product must be made a requirement at our school not only for the benefit of the students, but also for the benefit of the parents and staff!

Picture from fitbit.com


Live Tracking

  • Live tracking of every vehicle with the device installed.
  • Exact location of the school vehicle with students.

Geofencing

  • Alerts & notifies the users when user enters or leaves virtually set premises.
  • All the parents and staff can be notified about the school vehicle's location.

Data maintenance & Reminders

  • Saves drivers' data as well as the vehicle's maintenance data and reminds them of its updates.
  • Helps keep a track on the vehicle's service.

Identify unauthorized usage of vehicles

  • Alerts & notifies admins when the engine switch is turned on or off.
  • The admins are alerted of any use of vehicles.
